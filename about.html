About Playdate

Sports are better with others. Playdate helps you find athletes in your city who share your sport, your schedule, and your drive to get out and play.

How it works
1

Create your profile

Complete your Player Card so the right athletes can find you.

2

Connect with athletes in your community.

Browse the map and find athletes near you.

3

Schedule a Playdate

Pick a time, send an invite, and show up to play.
