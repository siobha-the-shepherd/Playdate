About Playdate

Sports are better with others. Playdate helps you find athletes in your city who share your sport, your schedule, and your drive to get out and play.

How it works
1

Create your profile

Tell us your sport, skill level, and neighbourhood.

2

Find athletes nearby

Browse the map and connect with athletes near you.

3

Schedule a Playdate

Pick a time, send an invite, and show up to play.

4

Build your network

Grow your local sports community one game at a time.
